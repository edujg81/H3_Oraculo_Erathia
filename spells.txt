as &lt;spell&gt; . Each spell has an effect that can be improved upon by Empowering ( &lt;power&gt; ) it. This makes the effect of the spell stronger, at the expense of using extra cards to do so. In addition to their regular effects, any spell can also be discarded to provide +1 &lt;power&gt; . Spells are gained by Searching the Spell deck, either the Mage Guild , by visiting fields or by any other effect that lets the player do the Search . Levels &para; Basic Spells &para; Basic Spell Cards have the symbol of their School in one of its corners. Expert Spells &para; Expert Spell Cards have the symbol of their School in all of its corners. Schools &para; All Spell Cards belong to one School: Air, Earth, Fire, or Water. The sole exception to this rule is the Magic Arrow spell, which can belong to any School (its School needs to be chosen at the time of casting it). Hero specialties are not considered Spells, even though some of them have a School of Magic.
